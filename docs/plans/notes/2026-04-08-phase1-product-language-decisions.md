# Phase 1 Product Language Decisions

Date: 2026-04-08

- Public product framing: investigation-driven policy research workspace
- User-facing label: Investigations
- Internal/backend object retained for now: Collections
- Primary workflow: Search -> save to investigation -> analyze -> synthesize
- Composer remains available, but is positioned as a downstream / advanced drafting workflow rather than the homepage product story
- Assistant remains labeled Assistant for Phase 1; a Copilot rename can be evaluated later after the investigation workflow is clearer
- Reports remain available, but are not treated as a primary top-nav destination in the MVP-first framing
